# Thank-You / Follow-Up After Interview

Send within 24 hours of the interview. One per interviewer if you have their names.

---

## After a Technical Interview

```
Subject: Thank you — [Role] interview

Hi [Name],

Thank you for the interview today. I really enjoyed [one specific thing from the conversation —
a problem you discussed, a question they asked, something about the team they shared].

[Optional: one sentence connecting something from the conversation to why you're excited about the role.]

Looking forward to next steps!

[Your name]
```

---

## After a Final Round

```
Subject: Thank you — [Role] final round

Hi [Name],

Thank you for the time today. I came away from the conversations feeling even more excited
about [Company / team / product area].

[One genuine, specific detail from the day — a topic discussed, a team insight, something
you learned that changed how you see the role.]

I'd love to join the team and hope to hear from you soon.

[Your name]
```

---

## Rules

- Always send within 24 hours
- One email per person if you have multiple interviewers' emails
- Be specific — generic thank-yous are forgettable
- Do not ask about the outcome in the thank-you email
