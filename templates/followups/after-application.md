# Follow-Up After Applying

Send 1–2 weeks after submitting, if you have a recruiter contact.
One message only.

---

```
Subject: Following up — [Role Title] at [Company]

Hi [Name],

I wanted to follow up on my application for [Role] that I submitted on [date].
I'm genuinely excited about [one specific thing — the team's work, a recent product launch,
the mission area, etc.].

Please let me know if there's anything else I can provide to support my application.

Best,
[Your name]
```
