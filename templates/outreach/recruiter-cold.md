# Recruiter Cold Outreach Template

Use only after applying through the official channel.
Keep it specific and short — recruiters get a lot of messages.

---

## LinkedIn Message to Recruiter

```
Hi [Name],

I applied for [Role] at [Company] on [date] and wanted to reach out directly.

I'm particularly drawn to [one specific thing about the team/product/mission — not generic].
[Optional: one sentence connecting your background to the role.]

Happy to share anything that would be helpful. Thanks for your time!

[Your name]
```

---

## Email to Recruiter (if email is available)

```
Subject: [Your Name] — Application for [Role Title]

Hi [Name],

I wanted to follow up on my application for [Role] submitted on [date].

[One sentence about what drew you to this specific role or team — be specific.]
[One sentence about the most relevant thing in your background.]

I'd love the opportunity to chat if you have availability.

Best,
[Your name]
[Phone if appropriate]
[LinkedIn URL]
```

---

## Rules

- Only one follow-up — do not send multiple messages
- Reference the specific role, not just "any openings"
- Always be specific about why this company or team (not generic praise)
- Never ask to "skip the process" or "bypass the ATS"
