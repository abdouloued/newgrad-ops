# Referral DM Template — LinkedIn

Use this for reaching out to alumni or connections at a target company.
Keep it short. Be specific. Make it easy to say yes.

---

## Connection Request (≤300 characters)

```
Hi [Name],

I'm a [year] [School] [Major] student interested in [role type] roles at [Company].
I noticed you [went to [School] / worked at [shared company]] — would love to connect!

[Your name]
```

---

## Follow-up Message After Connecting (for InMail or DM)

```
Hi [Name],

Thanks for connecting! I'm currently applying to [specific role] at [Company] and would
love to hear about your experience there if you have 15–20 minutes.

Specifically, I'm curious about [one specific question — team culture, onboarding,
what a typical project looks like, etc.].

No pressure at all — I appreciate any time you can spare.

[Your name]
```

---

## Referral Ask (only after a real conversation)

```
Hi [Name],

Really appreciated our conversation last week. I went ahead and applied for [Role].

If you feel comfortable submitting a referral, I'd be very grateful — but completely
understand if you'd rather not.

[Link to role if helpful]

Either way, thanks so much for your time.

[Your name]
```

---

## Rules

- Never send the referral ask before having a real conversation
- One personalized message per person — no copy-paste blasts
- Always follow up once, then let it go
- If they refer you, send a thank-you regardless of outcome
