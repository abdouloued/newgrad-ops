# Code of Conduct

This project exists to help students get their first tech offer. Be the kind
of contributor you'd want reviewing your resume.

- Be respectful in issues, PRs, and reviews — assume good faith.
- No harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks.
- Keep data contributions honest: don't add interview information you can't
  cite a public source for, and don't pad problem lists with guesses.
- Disagreements about technical content (e.g. whether a pattern tag is right)
  belong in the PR thread with reasoning, not in personal terms.

Violations can be reported by opening an issue or contacting the maintainers
directly. Maintainers may remove comments, commits, or contributors that
don't follow this code.

Adapted from the spirit of the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/).
